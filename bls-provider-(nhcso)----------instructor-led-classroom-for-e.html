AHA BLS Provider for NHCSO Deputies – First Responder Focused This American Heart Association BLS Provider course is designed specifically for NHCSO Deputies, emphasizing first responder skills in high-stress environments. This training equips participants with the knowledge and hands-on practice necessary to perform life-saving interventions during critical emergencies. Course Highlights: Emphasis on first responder BLS skills relevant to law enforcement High-performance CPR techniques for adult, child, and infant patients Use of an AED and advanced airway considerations in the field Team-based resuscitation strategies for emergency response Scenario-based training tailored for law enforcement situations Special Note: This AHA program is sponsored by the department.
