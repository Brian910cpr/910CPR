The Dates/Times below are for a Hands-On Session (Step 2) for an AHA online course. * If you need Step 1: Once you choose a date to test, you will be offered Class Type selections. (e.g., First Aid CPR/AED, Pediatric First Aid CPR, etc.) If you are seeking a Merchant Mariner First Aid Certification, add both TOTAL and the AMERHA-216 options. Bloodborne Pathogens can be added to any selection. The Heartsaver Online courses are taught in a blended learning format. Students must complete the online portion, followed by the hands-on skills session conducted by an American Heart Association Training Center Instructor. Upon successful completion, students will receive a Heartsaver Course Completion Card, valid for 2 years. The AHA’s Heartsaver courses are designed for individuals with little or no medical training who require first aid and/or CPR training for job requirements, regulatory compliance (OSHA), or personal preparedness. The AHA's Heartsaver Online Courses Are Available in the Following Options: First Aid with CPR/AED TOTAL ( Click for course details) Comprehensive course designed to meet OSHA and other regulatory requirements. Includes all available course topics. Meets US Coast Guard Elementary First Aid training requirements. Estimated Online Course Time: 1.25 to 3 hours. First Aid with CPR/AED BASIC ( Click for course details) Flexible course with optional topics. Estimated Online Course Time: 1.25 to 3 hours. Pediatric First Aid and CPR/AED Focuses on infants and children (adult training is optional). Ideal for childcare workers, teachers, and camp counselors. Estimated Online Course Time: 1.5 to 3 hours. CPR/AED Only Teaches how to perform CPR and use an AED safely and effectively. Estimated Online Course Time: 1 to 1.5 hours. First Aid Only For individuals needing a First Aid completion card to meet job, regulatory, or other requirements. Estimated Online Course Time: 1.5 hours. Bloodborne Pathogens Designed for workers likely to encounter bloodborne pathogens, including healthcare workers, security personnel, and tattoo artists. Estimated Online Course Time: 1 hour. This course can be completed 100% online. Upon completion of the online portion, bring your Certificate of Completion to class or email it to info@910cpr.com.
